What is Kamatamare Sanuki?

Kamatamare Sanuki is a professional football club based in Kagawa Prefecture, located on the island of Shikoku in Japan.

The club competes in the Japanese football league system and represents the local community of Kagawa.

Club Name

The name "Kamatamare" comes from Sanuki udon, a famous local noodle dish. It reflects the club’s strong connection to local culture and hometown pride.

Home Stadium

Kamatamare Sanuki plays home matches at Marugame Stadium, one of the largest stadiums in Shikoku.

Watching a match here offers a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, especially for first-time visitors.

Why Watch a Match?

  • A local football experience, different from big-city clubs
  • Friendly supporters and an open atmosphere
  • A chance to enjoy football as part of your trip to Kagawa
