Marugame Stadium – Stadium & Access

This page helps first-time visitors get to Marugame Stadium and enjoy matchday smoothly. (Updated: 2025)

Quick Summary

  • Stadium: Marugame Stadium (Kagawa Prefectural Marugame Stadium)
  • Best for visitors: Arrive early, especially for weekend matches.
  • What to prepare: Water, rain gear, and a plan to leave with the crowd.

How to Get There

Open Marugame Stadium in Google Maps

Most visitors come by train + bus/taxi/walk. Driving is also possible, but parking can be crowded.

Option A: Train (Recommended)

  1. Take a train to Marugame Station (JR Yosan Line).
  2. From Marugame Station, go to the stadium by:
    • Bus: (Example) approx. 15–25 minutes depending on traffic.
    • Taxi: (Example) approx. 15 minutes.
    • Walk: (Example) 40–60 minutes (good weather only).

Option B: By Car

  • Parking is available, but can fill up on popular matchdays.
  • If you drive, arrive early and follow staff instructions.
  • After the match, leaving can take time due to traffic.

Matchday Tips (First-Time Visitors)

Arrive early

  • Recommended arrival: 60–90 minutes before kickoff.
  • This gives you time for tickets, food, photos, and finding your seat.

Food & drinks

  • Many matches have food stands (stadium gourmet).
  • Bring cash and/or a card depending on the vendor. (It can vary.)
  • In summer, hydration matters. Seriously.

Weather

  • Bring rain gear in rainy season.
  • In hot months, a hat and sunscreen help a lot.

Leaving after the match

  • Crowds move at once. If you want a smoother exit, consider waiting 10–15 minutes.
  • If you need to catch a train, plan your departure in advance.
